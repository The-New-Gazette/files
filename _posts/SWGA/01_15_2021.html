STATEWIDE

  1. Gov. Brian Kemp delivered his State of the State address yesterday. Kemp stated there would be no new budget cuts or tax increases this year and that the state did not have to tap into the revenue reserve funds to cover state government costs. The upcoming year’s priorities include a “rural innovation fund” and reforming GA’s citizen’s arrest law. Read more.

SWGA

  1. Southwest Public Health District 8-2 seeks temporary part-time nurses and healthcare workers to meet C-19 testing and vaccination demand. Workers are needed to staff the specimen sites, vaccination sites, and call centers. The department offers $15/hour for healthcare workers and $45/hour for Registered Nurses. Read more.

DECATUR COUNTY

  1. Bainbridge Public Safety responded to a fire at the Willis Park Hotel Building on Wednesday evening. BPS extinguished the fire, and the cause is under investigation. Building owner Tyler Thomas stated the hotel could recover but that the opening date will be delayed. Read more.
