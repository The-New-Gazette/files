IMPORTANT

  1. GA Secretary of State Brad Raffensperger has moved the Dec. 1 Public Service Commission election to Jan. 5 to coincide with the U.S. Senate runoff races. The voter registration deadline is Dec. 7. Read more.

    More: You can check your voter status here or request an absentee ballot here.

  2. The National Hurricane Center is now monitoring three separate systems. Tropical Storm Eta is near Gainesville, FL, moving across North FL towards the Atlantic. Tropical Storm Theta is moving east-northeast over the eastern Atlantic and forecast to weaken over the weekend. Disturbance 1 in the central Caribbean has a 90% chance of storm formation over the next 5 days. No significant impacts are currently forecast for SWGA. Read more.

SWGA

  1. A fox recently tested positive for rabies after attacking a man in Thomasville. Thomas Co. Health Department and the Department of Natural Resource officials remind SWGA that rabies is common in our local wildlife population and emphasize keeping vaccinations up to date for pets and livestock. Read more.

    Related: St. Francis Wildlife has additional advice on what to do if you find a wild animal here.

DECATUR COUNTY

  1. The Decatur Co. Sheriff’s Office is investigating a vandalism incident near the intersection of Salem Church and Old Whigham roads in Climax on Nov. 5. The Decatur County Citizens Against Crime group is offering a $2K reward for information leading to the arrest and conviction of the person(s) involved. Read more.

    Action: If you have information regarding this incident, you can contact Investigator Adam Mobley at 229-400-8011, Chief Investigator Elijah McCoy at 229-400-8004, or leave an anonymous tip.
