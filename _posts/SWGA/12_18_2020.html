TRI-STATE AREA

  1. The Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission has paused commercial and recreational harvesting of wild oysters from Apalachicola Bay until 2025. The five-year harvest suspension is part of a $20 million restoration effort to rebuild the Bay’s declining oyster population. The ban will run from Feb 1, 2021 - Dec 31, 2025, but can be lifted sooner if restoration efforts are successful. Read more.

SWGA

  1. Phoebe Putney Memorial Hospital in Albany received 6,000 doses yesterday in its first shipment of COVID-19 vaccines. Albany was one of the most severely affected hotspots in the world during the pandemic's early months. Vaccinations will occur in tiers, starting with front-line health care workers. Phoebe’s head of emergency medicine, Dr. James Black, was the first to be vaccinated. Read more.
