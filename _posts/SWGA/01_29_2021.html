STATEWIDE

  1. The first half of the 2021 fiscal year was the most profitable in GA Lottery history. Gov. Kemp announced Thursday that the lottery generated over $731 million in profits between Jul and Dec of 2020. Profits from the lottery help pay for specific educational programs, including the state’s Pre-K program and the HOPE Scholarship program. Read more.

SWGA

  1. The Decatur Co Sheriff’s Office arrested a Cairo man for operating an unlicensed racetrack in Climax. Dan Cobb turned himself in after the State Fire Marshal’s Office issued arrest warrants. Warrants were issued after an accident led to death during a race at the unlicensed track last Oct. Cobb also lacked appropriate insurance coverage and did not have an ambulance staged for the event. Read more.

  2. Seminole Co is developing Project Beautification 2023 to inspire citizens to clean up the county. The two-year project will include free trash dump and pick-up days and scheduled clean-up projects for all areas of Donalsonville and Seminole Co. The project’s Adopt A Mile program is accepting participants now while specific plans and details are developed for the 2023 project. Individuals or groups interested in keeping an adopted mile clean can contact Virginia Harrison at 229-400-3772. Read more.
