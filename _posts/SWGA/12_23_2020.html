News

SWGA

  1. A cold front is moving in for Christmas Eve, bringing heavy rain and gusty winds. There is a marginal risk for severe storms and wind gusts over 60 mph. Counties near the FL state line should stay alert for tornado threats. Read more.

  2. Doses of Moderna’s C-19 vaccine have arrived at Seminole Co. Public Health. Chief of Staff Dr. Jeff Holley and Respiratory Therapist Pam Register were the first to be vaccinated on Tue. Other frontline workers at Donalsonville Hospital are next in line, followed by Seminole Manor Nursing Home residents. A second dose is required within 28 days for full effectiveness. Read more.

  3. Vaccinations with the Pfizer vaccine are underway for employees at Archbold network hospitals, including Mitchell and Brooks Co. Hospitals as well as the Medical Center in Thomasville. Read more. Grady General Hospital and the Grady Co. Health Department began administering the vaccine to healthcare workers on Tues. Read more.

DECATUR COUNTY

  1. According to GA’s new C-19 vaccine dashboard, 4 locations in Decatur Co. have ordered C-19 vaccine doses. Bainbridge Pharmacy, Briarcliff Pharmacy, the Decatur Co. Health Department and MainStreet Family Care have placed orders. MainStreet has requested the most doses so far - 975 Pfizer and 1000 Moderna. You can keep up with vaccine orders, distribution, and administered doses here.
