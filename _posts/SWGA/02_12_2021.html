News

STATEWIDE

  1. The GA House and Senate approved changes to this year’s budget and sent House Bill 80 to the governor for his signature. The plan restores $567 million to education funding and includes $1K bonuses for state employees who make less than $80K annually. $27 million is designated for the Department of Public Health for vaccine efforts and administrative staffing. Read more.

SWGA

  1. 60K doses of COVID-19 vaccine have been administered across fourteen southwest GA counties. Jacqueline Jenkins of Southwest GA Health District 8-2 reports that no one in the district has experienced serious vaccine issues. Read more.

    Related: 1,181 Seminole Co residents have received the vaccine as of Feb 10. Read more.

    More: Health District 8-2 is holding a Facebook event today, Feb 12, at 3:30 to answer questions about the C-19 vaccine. Read more.

DECATUR CO.

  1. Construction is underway on the new Harbor Freight store in Bainbridge. The discount tool store will be on Tallahassee Hwy next to Tractor Supply and will open this summer. The location will bring 25 - 30 new jobs to our area. Read more.
