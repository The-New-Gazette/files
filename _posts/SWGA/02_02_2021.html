SWGA

  1. The Grady Co History Museum is fundraising with a Chicken Pilau Dinner on Thursday, Feb 4. Tickets are available for $8 at Center Drugs, Drew Oil, Cox Pharmacy, Miss Myrt’s, and at the Museum in Cairo. Dinner will be available for pick up at 101 N Broad St in Cairo from 4 pm - 7 pm. Proceeds benefit the Grady County History Museum.

  2. Bainbridge Public Safety (BPS) warns residents that coyote mating season is underway until March. BPS advises keeping pets indoors between dusk and dawn, securing trash cans, and preventing coyotes from having access to food (including small animals and pets) near your home. Read more about coyotes here.
