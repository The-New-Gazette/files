ELECTIONS

  1. GA voters have until Friday, Nov. 6 at 5 pm to check and cure absentee ballot status. Your county elections office should contact you if your ballot was rejected for any reason. Read more.

    More You can also check the status of your ballot on the Secretary of State’s My Voter Page. If you need help in GA curing your ballot call the Voter Protection Hotline at 888-730-5816.

  2. GA voters approved two constitutional amendments and one statute on Tuesday. Amendment 1 ensures state fees and taxes collected for a specific purpose must be spent for that purpose. Amendment 2 prohibits state and local governments from using sovereign immunity to avoid citizen lawsuits. House Bill 244 passed to help increase affordable housing in small cities and rural areas. Read more.
