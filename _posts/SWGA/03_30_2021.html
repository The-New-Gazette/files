SWGA

  1. More jobs are coming to southwest GA. As a result of their new relationship with Mars-Wrigley, Danimer Scientific plans to invest $700M to expand Bainbridge manufacturing operations. DECATUR COUNTY

  1. The Bainbridge-Decatur Marshal’s Office is notifying property owners of code violations by placing signs on properties. The yellow signs serve as a violation notice, and citations will be issued if code violations are not remedied by the compliance date. The Marshall’s office encourages all residents to proactively remove junk cars, trash, and abandoned structures, mow the grass, and trim the bushes. Read more.

  2. The Decatur County Election Board recently voted to make hand audits a routine part of the post-election process in Decatur County. According to Board Chairman Keith Sellars, the decision was made to “further institute trust in our election process” and prepare election workers for potential state-ordered audits. Poll workers recently conducted an audit on the March 16th Special Election, which accounted for an anomaly on a single ballot. All audits are open to the public. Read more.
