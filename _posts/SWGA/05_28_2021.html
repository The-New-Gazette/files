DECATUR COUNTY

  1. July 1 is the deadline for Decatur County homeowners to appeal this year’s property assessment. Many residents have seen a rise in their property taxes due to the inflated real estate market. The GA State Department of Audits mandates an increase in property values when there are enough home sales within the county at rates higher than Assessors’ values. To appeal your current property valuation visit the Decatur Co. Tax Assessors Office and turn in the appeal form for property owners. Read more.

Spotlight

Entomologists at the University of GA are asking the people of GA to help track the spread of an invasive bee. The sculptured resin bee, a native of China and Japan, was first spotted in North Carolina in 1994. The bees take over the nests of native carpenter bees and use kudzu as a food source. The resin bees are similar in size to carpenter bees but have a connected thorax and abdomen with raised bands. If you spot the tiny invaders, you can submit photos and findings here through the iNaturalist website. Learn more.
