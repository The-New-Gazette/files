  1. Public hearings on proposed changes to city ordinances are today at 6:30 pm in Bainbridge City Hall. City staff have proposed changes to the cemetery ordinance, tree ordinance, and nuisance ordinance and have proposed a new blight tax ordinance. If the new rule passes, properties officially identified as maintained in a blighted condition will be subject to an increased ad valorem tax.
