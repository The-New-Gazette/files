Spotlight

Some Decatur County residents have or will receive postcards and qualification questionnaires from the U.S. District Court for the Middle District of Georgia over the next few months. This is not a scam.

The Court is qualifying the approximately 75K prospective jurors in the 70-county district, a process that is completed every four years. While the information request is legitimate and a response is required, the questionnaire is not a county jury summons. Find out more on their website, call 478-752-2617, or email at juryclerk@gamd.uscourts.gov.

If you have questions about what you’ve received, you can also call the Decatur Co. Clerk’s Office at 229-248-3025.
