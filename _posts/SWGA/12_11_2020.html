TRI-STATE AREA

  1. Publix recalls holiday cookie platters sold in FL, GA, AL, TN, SC, NC, and VA. The 20oz packages contain undeclared pecans. Consumers with nut allergies can return the platters with lot code W34326 to the place of purchase for a full refund or call the company at 1-800-433-9100. Read more.

STATEWIDE

  1. Gov. Kemp announced Wed that GA would begin COVID-19 vaccine distribution soon, starting with health care workers and nursing home residents. A combination of Pfizer and Moderna vaccines will be distributed, quantity currently unknown. Kemp urged residents to continue social distancing and wearing masks since it will take several months to vaccinate the general public. Read more.
