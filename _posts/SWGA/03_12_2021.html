SWGA

  1. City of Albany officials reported a major spill of untreated sewage occurred at the Joshua Street Water Pollution Control Plant earlier this month. Due to high rainfall and high flows in the Flint River, the discharge was carried downstream and dissipated quickly. According to city officials, no drinking water systems were affected. Read more.

    More: View Flint Riverkeeper Gordon Rogers’ statement in response to the spill here.

    Context: Separating sewage and stormwater has been a long-term issue for Albany, and the city is facing a $234 million bill to update the outdated sewer system infrastructure. Read more.

DECATUR CO.

  1. A new weather camera has been installed at the Earle May Boat Basin in Bainbridge, providing Decatur Co residents access to real-time local weather information. The WeatherSTEM system includes radar, lifestyle forecasts, a daily almanac, current weather conditions, weather safety alerts, and educational weather content. You can follow the Bainbridge WeatherSTEM Facebook page, download the mobile apps, or visit the website.
