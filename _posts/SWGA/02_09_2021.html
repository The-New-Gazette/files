GEORGIA

  1. Clinical trials for the Moderna COVID-19 vaccine are now open to adolescents. In GA, two companies will conduct trials on children aged 12 to 17. Tekton Research in Chamblee is accepting applications for volunteers, and Clinical Research Atlanta will enroll soon. Read more.

    Related: The C-19 vaccine will soon be available at Publix pharmacies near us. Eligible Leon Co residents can sign up starting tomorrow. Appointments at the Publix in Thomasville are currently booked, but you can check back for availability here.

SWGA

  1. An Early Co family is asking for help from the community. Early Co. High School junior Tyler Winns was diagnosed with bone cancer last month. He will be commuting to the Scottish Rite Children’s Hospital in Atlanta for chemotherapy treatments and needs financial support for travel and housing expenses. Read more.

  2. The UGA Pecan Extension reminds growers to check pecan trees for beetle activity. Ambrosia beetle activity usually peaks in late Feb to mid-Mar in south GA. The extra rain this winter could lead to flooding that makes young trees more vulnerable to attack. Read more.
