News

IMPORTANT

  1. HSV Hospital temporarily expanded the C-19 vaccination clinic at John Hunt Park to vaccinate an additional 1,000 people a day. The clinic is already distributing 1,000 vaccines each day, bringing the total to an additional 5,000 this week as part of a larger initiative from the Alabama Department of Public Health. People ages 65+ and frontline essential workers are eligible for the vaccine, and appointments must be made in advance. The expanded clinic will run from Feb 8-12 from 7 am to 6 pm. Read more.

    More: Signup for the HSV Hospital vaccine waitlist here.

NORTH ALABAMA

  1. The new Madison County Service Center will open March 1st at N Memorial Parkway and Oakwood Avenue. The Service Center will include several departments, including the Tax Assessor, Tax Collector, and License Director. The Madison County Commission will meet on Feb 17th to announce which satellite offices will be moving from the courthouse. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. The SEC gymnastics championship will be held in HSV at the Von Braun Center’s Propst Arena on March 20th. The event was originally scheduled to be in New Orleans but was relocated due to C-19 restrictions that severely limited the number of fans attending. Read more.

  2. HSV Hospital will resume elective surgeries soon based on the procedure's urgency, starting with patients at Women’s and Children’s Hospital. The hospital’s VP of operations, Tracy Doughty, says the hospital has consolidated the C-19 units to open up room for other patients and move staff flexibly among departments. Read more.
