IMPORTANT

  1. There are 41 days left to apply for an Absentee Ballot for the Nov. 3 General Election. The AL Secretary of State's office has released sample ballots for each county. View Madison County's ballot.

  2. The general election on Nov. 3 will include a vote on passing Amendment 4 to begin the process of simplifying the AL constitution. The AL constitution is currently the longest in the world. Passage would allow the Legislative Services Agency to propose a simplified version for future ratification by voters. Read more.

NORTH ALABAMA

  1. Construction on U.S. Hwy 231 over Brindlee mountain is almost complete. The two bridges could be finished within the next three weeks, depending upon the weather. Each bridge will have two lanes for traffic and shoulders on either side. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. UAH has renovated and expanded historic Morton Hall for the 21st century. The facility has new building systems, 30% more space for classrooms and faculty, and a new Black Box Theater. The expansion includes new outdoor green space and the ability to serve as large capacity tornado shelters. Read more.
