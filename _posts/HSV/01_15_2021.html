  1. Traditional learning students in HSV and Madison City Schools will return to full-time, in-classroom learning next Tuesday, Jan 19. Spotlight

HSV City Council introduced and passed a resolution last night to rename the Berachah Campus to The Dr. Robert Shurney Legacy Center in honor of the late NASA engineer and his many contributions to the U.S. Space Program.

Dr. Shurney was one of the first African Americans to work in the Space Program and helped develop the Saturn V rocket and lunar rover designs. He provided weightlessness training for the Mercury Astronauts, worked on the Apollo program, and coordinated components for the U.S. human spaceflight program. After 36 years of government service, Dr. Shurney continued to serve his community in retirement through lectures, fundraising, and judging science fairs.
