STATEWIDE

  1. AL has the highest C-19 positivity rate and the lowest vaccination rate in the nation. Over 11,500 Alabamians have died of the disease, and over 1,250 people are hospitalized in the state. The rolling average of daily new cases has jumped over 265% in the last two weeks. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. Teachers of the Year from across the nation have signed a joint statement condemning American Airlines for discriminating against a colleague at HSV International Airport. The alleged discrimination occurred on Monday as several teachers were departing the state after attending Space Camp. According to the joint statement, American Airlines representatives refused to accommodate two Asian American Pacific Islander teachers after a scheduled flight was canceled but immediately accommodated a white male teacher just moments later. The airline is conducting an investigation. Read more.

REDSTONE ARSENAL

  1. Redstone Arsenal reinstated full masking requirements on Thursday. Although C-19 rates are low on the installation, arsenal officials decided to reinstate mask requirements based on the CDC indicators for substantial community transmission. All employees and guests must wear face coverings on the installation, regardless of vaccination status. Read more.
