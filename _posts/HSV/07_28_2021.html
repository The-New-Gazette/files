NORTH ALABAMA

  1. The state rested its case against Limestone County Sheriff Blakely, who is facing theft and ethics charges related to his personal use of department money and campaign funds. One juror in the case was released due to a spousal relationship with an inmate that said his release was tied to his wife voting not guilty. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. Local school officials have provided updates on mask mandates.

  • HSV City School officials announced that masks will be required for everyone 2 years and older in the upcoming school year. HSV City Board Members made the decision based on rising C-19 case numbers and discussions with hospital administrators. Read more.

  • Madison City Schools announced that masks will be optional for students and teachers in the upcoming school year. The schools will continually monitor health information and follow orders if they are told to mandate masks by state or local government. Read more.

    More: The latest C-19 data from HSV Hospital can be reviewed here.

  1. Site preparation is underway for the revitalization of Joe Davis Stadium off of Memorial Pkwy. Huntsville City Administrator John Hamilton expects contractors to start construction in earnest in the next 45 days, with the full renovation to take around 12 months. The hope is that the stadium could be ready for a minor league soccer team by the 2023 season. Read more.
