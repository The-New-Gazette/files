IMPORTANT

  1. HSV health officials are asking everyone to take serious precautions over the holidays to help reduce C-19 spread. Here are some key takeaways from yesterday’s HSV-area C-19 update:
  • Crestwood Hospital received 500 doses of the newly FDA-approved Moderna vaccine on Tuesday and has started offering it to staff and should continue to receive weekly shipments.
  • HSV Hospital has more than 300 staff on leave with C-19 or quarantined due to exposure leaving the hospital understaffed as cases rise. Crestwood has 48 staff on leave, and 19 HEMSI paramedics are on leave.
  • Inpatients with C-19 has gone up 61% since the beginning of the month.
  • 18,093 confirmed cases resulting in 160 deaths in Madison County.
  • You can find more data at Bamatracker and HSV Hospital stats.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. About one million pounds of food was distributed at the Food Bank of North Alabama’s downtown center in the last month. Food banks around the area are looking for food donations to continue to provide to people in need. Read more.

REDSTONE ARSENAL

  1. Gov. Ivey spoke with Pentagon officials to pitch the Redstone Arsenal for the new Space Command Headquarters' location. Military officials will select from 6 possible locations early next year, and Gov. Ivey said she is ready to give them a warm welcome in HSV. Read more.

    Context: The other sites being considered are Kirkland Air Force Base (New Mexico), Offut Air Force Base (Nebraska), Port San Antonio (Texas), Peterson Air Force Base (Colorado), and Patrick Air Force Base (Florida).
