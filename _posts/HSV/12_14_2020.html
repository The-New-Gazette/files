HUNTSVILLE

  1. HSV International Airport has completed Taxiway C and has implemented new technology for TSA to minimize touchpoints during the pandemic. The addition of Taxiway C will promote safer runway crossings, and the new computed tomography scanner will allow TSA officers to scan bags hands-free. Read more.

  2. HSV Virtual Academy can return to online platforms following the cybersecurity attack but should not use the HCS network or HSC devices. Elementary students resume online classes today, and secondary students will resume as soon as teachers receive new devices next week. High school students are slated to receive new devices in January. Meanwhile, the district will have remote learning days on Dec 21 and 22. Read more.

Global Ties Alabama is looking for 18-29 year-olds to represent the U.S. at the 2021 World’s Fair in Dubai. You can apply to be either a Youth Ambassador or a Cultural Performer. Youth Ambassadors will be examples of the United States’ cultural diversity.
