HUNTSVILLE

  1. HSV Hospital for Women and Children has a new, state-of-the-art pediatric ambulance. The new vehicle was fully funded by an anonymous donor and contains specialized equipment for transporting patients aged 23 weeks - 21 years from area hospitals to the Women and Children’s center. The Kids Care ambulance fleet transports approximately 700 patients a year. Read more.

  2. Plans are in development to improve safety and traffic flow at the I-565 Interchange and Memorial Pkwy. The project is expected to take years to complete and cost over $200M. The roadways constructed in the ’80s are currently carrying over double the planned capacity for traffic. Read more.

  3. Local and national artists are invited to submit qualifications for two large-scale public art installations at the new HSV City Hall complex. Arts Huntsville announced the national Request for Qualifications on Thursday for installations in the City Hall entry lobby and above the grand staircase. Additional awards will also go exclusively to local artists for up to five public pieces through the complex. Read more.
