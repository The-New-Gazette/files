MADISON COUNTY

  1. As of now, masks will be optional in all Madison County school districts this fall. HSV City, Madison County, and Madison City Schools have released back-to-school plans and C-19 precautions for the upcoming school year. Despite recommendations from the American Academy of Pediatrics, none will require masks inside schools. Read more.

    Related: According to HSV Hospital President Tracy Doughty, C-19 hospitalizations have nearly doubled in the last two weeks, as vaccination rates have slowed. Watch more.

  2. Phillip Rigsby, an HSV pharmacist, announced his candidacy for AL State Representative District 25. The 25th District includes Madison and Limestone Counties. Rigsby will run in the May 24, 2022, Republican primary to replace State House Speaker Mac McCutcheon, who will not seek another term. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. FOLLOW UP: HSV City Council approved two police reform resolutions. The first resolution enhances the Huntsville Police Citizens Advisory Council (HPCAC), and the second addresses making police body camera footage available to the public. Last month, both resolutions were tabled and were then back on the docket this week and approved in the council meeting yesterday. Plans for enacting the resolutions are still being worked out. Read more.

  2. HSV police arrested a registered sex offender Thursday morning after an overnight standoff at a home on Thorntons Way Circle. Decatur Police sought the 29-year old suspect for seven felony charges. The standoff began around 5 pm Wednesday when HSV police attempted to serve the arrest warrant; the suspect surrendered peacefully and is booked into Morgan County Jail with a $695K bond. Read more.

CITY OF MADISON

  1. Madison City students return to school on August 4. Schools will resume normal activities, masks are not required, and a remote option will not be offered through MCS. Quarantine guidelines are still in effect for non-vaccinated individuals. Students can visit new teachers and classrooms at Open House on Aug 2nd. Read more.
