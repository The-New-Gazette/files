Spotlight

Steeped in local history, "The Corner Office" at 123-125 North Side Square has completed its extensive renovations and is poised to be a bustling cog in HSV's downtown again soon. 

The building Started as a dry-goods and furniture store in the late 1800s, since then it has had many different purposes and several architectural makeovers.

Most recently, the property was purchased by Crunkleton and Associates in 2019, who have restored the outer brick facade to its former glory. With the upper floors currently housing various offices, the ground floor will host several restaurants, including the new Jack Brown's Beer & Burger Joint.
