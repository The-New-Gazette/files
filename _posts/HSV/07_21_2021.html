  1. Data from the Alabama Department of Public Health shows that the number of C-19 patients in Alabama hospitals has increased by 130% since July 1. According to the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, Alabama saw the nation's fifth-highest C-19 test positivity rate. Read more.
