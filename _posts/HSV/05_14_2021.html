HSV Hospital begins vaccinating children ages 12+ today with the Pfizer C-19 vaccine. 44% of Madison County residents 16+ have received at least one dose, and 35% are now fully vaccinated, according to the HSV-Madison Co. Emergency Management Agency Director.   2. As construction begins on the 8,000 seat amphitheater at HSV’s MidCity district, details about its look and feel are emerging. The structure will have a Romanesque design and include an advanced sound system designed to entice musical acts. There will also be a dedicated park and a smaller performance venue for local musicians. Businesses nearby, including those outside of MidCity, are expected to see a revenue bump when the amphitheater opens in summer 2022. Read more.

    More: The MidCity District continues to expand. Still to come are Trader Joe’s, a pottery studio, and two new hotels. Read more.

Spotlight

The Rotary Club of Madison and volunteers planted 30K milkweed seeds and other pollinator plants in Dublin Park last fall to create a habitat for migrating Monarch butterflies. Master Gardeners, Girl Scouts, Boy Scouts, National Honor Society members, Rotary members, and other volunteers created the 10’ x 160’ Monarch Butterfly Habitat with donated seeds and supplies; the plot should be in full bloom by August. An additional habitat was built for the Madison Hospital Healing Garden. Habitats are also planned for all Madison City School campuses. Read more.
