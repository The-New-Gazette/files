Spotlight

HSV city officials are encouraging local groups to coordinate their donations for the homeless this season. Citizens can donate directly to agencies that work closely with the homeless population, including First Stop, The Salvation Army, New Futures, Manna House, and Downtown Rescue Mission. You can also call HPD’s Community Relations Unit at 256-722-7100 to get insight into ways to support the homeless in our city. Read more.
