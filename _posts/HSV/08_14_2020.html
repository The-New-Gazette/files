MADISON CO.

  1. The HSV-Madison Co. Public Library is offering free access to tutoring through the Homework Alabama program. Homework reviews and one-on-one help is available daily from 10 am to 11 pm. Tutoring and other virtual learning resources are available for all Madison Co. and HSV students with a library card. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. The Mazda-Toyota Partnership is increasing investment in the HSV facility by $830M for a total of over $2.3B. HSV Mayor Tommy Battle noted the increased investment would bring in an additional $4M in educational tax revenue. Construction continues on the HSV plant, and the company expects to hire more workers later this year. Auto production is scheduled to begin in 2021. Read more.

  2. Thirty-one private companies from the HSV metro area made the Inc. 5000 list for 2020. According to Inc., the aggregate impact produced by these companies is $1.3B and 2,405 jobs. The highest-ranking company in the HSV metro area this year is Redline Steel sitting at position 110 with an outstanding 3,215% growth. See the list.

CITY OF MADISON

  1. All Madison employees are receiving equal retirement benefits. The City Council unanimously approved a measure simplifying a two-tiered benefits system in hopes of improving recruitment and retention of employees. Previously some workers could not draw retirement before the age of 62 - now all employees can retire and receive benefits after 25 years of service. Read more.

REDSTONE ARSENAL

  1. The FBI is planning to relocate at least 1,500 employees and contract workers to Alabama before the end of 2021. Two FBI operations already have facilities on Redstone Arsenal. Construction of a new facility for the FBI is expected to be complete by the end of next year, with two more facilities awaiting funding. The relocation will occur independently of developments with the bureau’s Washington headquarters. Read more.

    Context: The FBI has had a presence in HSV for nearly 40 years. This Alabama expansion is part of the agency’s long-term realignment plan. Read more.
