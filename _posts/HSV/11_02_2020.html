HUNTSVILLE

  1. Recently elected and re-elected HSV officials will be sworn in today. The ceremony will be held privately at Mars Music Hall to reduce C-19 concerns. Those sworn in will include Mayor Tommy Battle, council member Devyn Keith, and council member John Meredith. This will be followed by the city council meeting later today to elect a leader. Read more

    More: You can watch the city council session, starting at 1 pm, here.

  2. There were 163 C-19 patients in the HSV Hospital system as of Friday, the highest number since August. The hospital has been at 100% capacity for a week with overflow patients in the ER. HSV Hospital Health System CEO David Spillers says he is concerned that the hospital will be too full to accommodate all C-19 patients and flu patients as winter approaches. Read more.

  3. NASA’s Marshall Space Flight Center will host the sixth annual HBCU/MSI Partnerships Meeting. The meeting providing updates on partnerships and opportunities for historically Black colleges and universities and other minority-serving institutions to engage in subcontracting with NASA. The meeting is open to the public and will be held online on Nov. 3 from 9 am to 1:30 pm. Read more.
