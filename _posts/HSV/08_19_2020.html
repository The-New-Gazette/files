NORTH ALABAMA

  1. Dr. Patricia Sims, the President of Drake State Community College, is the first African-American woman appointed to the AL Port Authority Board of Directors. The Port Authority handles more than 26 million tonnes of cargo, employs more than 150K people, and generates $25.4B each year. Once approved by the state Senate, Dr. Sims will represent the Northern District of AL and succeeds Al Stanley, whose term expired on July 31. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. Raytheon donated $4M to the Alabama School of Cyber Technology & Engineering (ASCTE) in HSV. The donation will boost the engineering and cybersecurity preparatory programs to meet the unique demands of a future tech-centric workforce. ASCTE is currently holding classes on Oakwood University's campus until the completion of a new school at Cummings Research Park. Read more.

  2. The new 3-story women's suffrage mural on Washington street was dedicated on the 100th anniversary of the 19th amendment. The dedication was held by Arts Huntsville and the Women's Economic Development Council of HSV, who coordinated and planned the project for the past two years. Read more.

  3. The Church Street Wine Shoppe is relocating to the storied Humphreys-Rogers House. The Victorian mansion built in 1848 and listed on the Historic Register will provide additional space for the 600-member wine club. It will also become the new headquarters for all five of the Church Street Family of restaurants in HSV. The new location will be known as Church Street on Gates. Read more (limited viewing access).

Spotlight

A new shake and tea bar is opening soon in Hampton Coveoffering loaded teas and healthy shakes. Cove Nutrition will be located at the Hampton Place shopping center off Highway 431. With an underlying goal of building community in Hampton Cove, owner, Casey Yarbrough, lauds Cove Nutrition as a "healthy alternative with a menu that includes meal replacement shakes, loaded energy teas, and specialty drinks."

Housed in a brightly lit and energetic 1,500 square foot space, Cove Nutrition is aiming to be open in early Q4, 2020. Read more.
