IMPORTANT

  1. Local paramedics are stretched thin by staffing shortages and population growth in Madison County. HEMSI, the primary ambulance service for HSV, the city of Madison, and Madison Co., is increasing pay and starting its own EMT academy to fill positions. Lack of paramedics has increased call dispatch and response times, making it harder to retain existing workers. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. The new Toyota Corolla Cross SUV will be the first vehicle manufactured at the HSV Mazda Toyota plant. The front-wheel-drive SUV gets an average of 36.2 miles per gallon. Production begins later this year; vehicles will are expected to be in dealer lots by Fall. The plant is still hiring and hopes to employ up to 4K people in 2021. Read more.

  2. The Rosa Parks Day Committee (RPC) of HSV-Madison Co. has formally requested the Department of Justice investigate the response tactics and policies of HPD. The RPC’s letter cited incidents of excessive force in response to lawful protests and mental health crises, as well as the city’s continued employment of William Darby. Read more.
