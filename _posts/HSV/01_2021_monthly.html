MADISON COUNTY

  1. Madison Co. Commission Chairman Dale Strong delivered the State of Madison County address this past week. Watch it here.

  2. Madison County gave its final defense against the state’s lawsuit filed for moving the Confederate monument from the courthouse. The state says the removal violated the Alabama Memorial Preservation Act of 2017. The Madison Co. Commission chose to fight the suit, stating that their request to move the monument received tacit approval when the request was not responded to within 90 days. They have challenged the $25k fine and contested provisions in the Alabama Memorial Preservation Act as unconstitutional. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. The Secretary of the Air Force announced Redstone Arsenal as the preferred location for the new U.S. Space Command headquarters. The current provisional headquarters are in Colorado Springs. The decision will not be made final until Spring 2023, after a required environmental impact analysis is made. Read more.

    Context: Here are the statements from Sen. Shelby and Mayor Battle.

    More: The entire Colorado congressional delegation appealed to the Biden administration to review the decision, saying President Trump intervened in the process, with some officials calling the move politically motivated and fiscally irresponsible. Read more.

  2. North AL Land Trust has pulled plans for a nature center on Monte Sano. A group of residents passionately opposed the addition of a new nature center on the property near Tollgate Rd. New locations are now being considered for the center, which the organization says is urgently needed to inform people about the value of conserving nature and wild spaces. Read more.

  3. Three UAH PD officers, including the Chief, have been dismissed after a teen was harassed last year. The young man who was stopped for a cracked taillight was inappropriately accused of having drugs and a dead prostitute in his car. After a thorough investigation, the officer, the Police Captain, and the Police Chief have all been fired. Meanwhile, UAH has initiated a national search for a new chief and established a Police Advisory Council. Read more.

  4. A federal lawsuit has been filed against the HSV city school board and five school system employees for extreme bullying of a special needs student. The suit alleges three students bullied and physically attacked an 8-year-old boy at Mountain Gap Elementary School in late 2016 and early 2017. Attorneys for the child’s mother asked for a jury trial and compensatory and punitive damages for the injuries the child received. Read more.
