News

IMPORTANT

  1. The deadline for requesting an absentee ballot for municipal elections is August 20. For those concerned about COVID-19 exposure, a statewide allowance was announced in July to vote absentee. However, you must submit an application five days before the election on August 25.

    Register: Incumbent Mayor Tommy Battle’s campaign website has provided thorough absentee voting instructions and the digital application.

    More: When the ballot is received in the mail, it must be completed and mailed back (postmarked on or before August 24), or dropped off in-person to Floor 1 of City Hall on or before August 25. Voters who go to the polls in person will find social distancing and regular disinfecting measures taking place. In-person voters should bring a black ballpoint pen.

NORTH ALABAMA

  1. TVA has rescinded its June order to layoff IT staff. As part of a company-wide restructuring in response to the COVID-19 pandemic, the power provider announced layoffs of IT workers and a plan to outsource their work. Newly appointed TVA leaders met with White House staff on Thursday and agreed to rescind all IT layoff notices. Read more.

  2. Decatur-based United Launch Alliance (ULA) has secured a $337M contract with the U.S. Space Force. ULA will provide rockets for 60% of Space Force launches through 2027, with the other 40% allocated to SpaceX. ULA’s Vulcan Centaur will contain HSV-built Blue Origin engines and was chosen based on the company's spotless record of 100% mission success over 140 launches. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. The HSV Police Citizens Advisory Council (HPCAC) launched a website for citizens and witnesses to submit video footage of the June protests. The council has also announced two times over the next two weeks, during which it will hear public testimony about the demonstrations. Read more

    More: The HPCAC recommends pre-registering for a time-slot if you want to speak before the council. You can register and upload videos at huntsvillepolicereview.com.
