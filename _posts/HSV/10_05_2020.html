HUNTSVILLE

  1. Boeing received a $249M contract modification from the US Missile Defense Agency for work in HSV. The modification kicks off a Service Life Extension Program to upgrade and repair core equipment used in the missile defense system. Read more.

    Context: Boeing’s Ground-based Midcourse Defense program, headquartered in HSV, has served to detect, track and intercept long-range missiles across the nation since 2004.

  2. Fall and Halloween festivities will look different due to C-19 this year. The City of HSV recommends not engaging in traditional trick-or-treating, trunk-or-treat events, group hayrides, or indoor haunted houses this Halloween. They have also outlined some additional tips and recommendations for staying safe while enjoying the season. Read more.
