MADISON CO.

  1. Impact Aid Forms, which help the three local school systems receive federal funding, are due Nov.13. The federal funds are scoped to support schools in areas with federally active populations and can be used for teacher salaries, equipment, after school programs, and more. Families should fill out the form if either parent or guardian is on active duty in the uniformed services, employed on federal properties, or lives in Federal Housing. Read more.

    Context: In the last fiscal year, the three local school systems received around $663k from Impact Aid.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. The Margarita Society announced that the annual Margarita Ball is canceled due to C-19. To continue its support to local charities, the Society is holding 3 toy drives to collect 3,400 toys and held a golf tournament earlier this week. Read more

  2. HSV STEAM Works will be closing on Dec 19th due to a sustained impact on their operations from C-19. The organization which provided a maker space and hands-on technology education had to greatly decrease their services and local school programs during the pandemic. Facilities will remain open through the 19th, and all existing commitments will be completed until they close. Read more.

  3. HSV police are investigating the shooting of two victims found in a car at the HSV Utilities Payment Center on Pulaski Pike. Police are still searching the area for a suspect they believe fled on foot. The victims have both been transferred to HSV hospital, and one is in critical condition. Police also secured the perimeter and have maintained a heightened presence at nearby Sonnie Hereford Elementary School. Read more.

Spotlight

The new amphitheater at MidCity will have a Romanesque design with a bowl shape inside to allow all attendees to hear the live music no matter where they are sitting. The venue will also be utilized as a park and feature events such as festivals and farmers' markets. The idea for the new community venue was sparked by the City of Huntsville’s audit of the local music industry and what could help make it flourish by London based consultants, Sound Diplomacy. Construction will begin in 2021, with an estimated completion date of January 2022. Read more.

You can learn more about the new amphitheater at huntsvilleamphitheater.com.
