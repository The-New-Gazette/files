NORTH ALABAMA

  1. A man confessed to the cold case killing of HSV resident Christopher Daily in 1995. In the case, Johnny Dwight Whited, a longtime suspect, called the Decatur Police on Wednesday to confess and was arrested and charged with murder later that day. Mr. Daily’s surviving family has been informed of the arrest of Mr. Whited. Read more.

  2. Alabama launched Revive Plus, a $200M grant program designed to support small businesses and organizations through C-19. Small businesses, non-profits, and faith-based organizations with 50 or fewer employees will be eligible to receive up to $20K. Applications are open from Nov. 23 – Dec. 4 at crf.alabama.gov. Read more.

MADISON CO.

  1. Alabama Attorney General filed a lawsuit against Madison County claiming the removal of the Confederate monument was illegal. The Commission attempted multiple legal means to relocate the monument over the past few months and finally did so after 90 days of no response from a state monument committee. Madison County Circuit Judge Donna Pate was assigned to the case and immediately recused herself. Read more.
