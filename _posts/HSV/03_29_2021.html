STATEWIDE

  1. Three-term Madison County Commission Chairman Dale Strong is scheduled to announce his campaign for the 5th Congressional District seat this morning. Strong is pursuing Rep. Mo Brooks’ seat in the Congress, which will only be vacant if Brooks’ run for the U.S. Senate succeeds. Strong is attempting to follow Brooks’ path from Madison County Commission to the U.S. Congress. Read more.

HUNTSVILLE

  1. The HSV City Council discussed the progress of a new co-response program for crisis intervention at a recent meeting. The program’s end goal is to send mental health professionals to scenes of mental crisis rather than police. Law enforcement agencies are undergoing training and certification in crisis intervention. Read more.

MADISON

  1. The iconic Roundhouse in downtown Madison is undergoing major renovations. The current structure, a replica of the original from the 1890s, was built by volunteers at the Madison Street Festival in 1986 but is falling into disrepair. The Roundhouse holds historical significance as it was used as the original town hall. Read more.

Spotlight

The Master Gardeners of North Alabama Inc. will hold the 2021 Plant Sale on April 24 from 8 am to 3 pm at Madison County Farmers Market. Admission is free, and there will be both heirloom and hybrid vegetables, herbs, and flowers. There will be raffle giveaways, pH soil testing, and an “Ask a Master Gardener” booth where you can learn the fundamentals of plant care and little-known solutions to plant problems. Read more.
